Hey Neighbor: Empowering NYC Renters
Hey Neighbor is a platform that shares insider details about NYC apartments, ensuring NYC renters find comfortable and safe living conditions. After experiencing the insane rental market in NYC, I developed this project while attending SVA MFAD. There is a real information disparity between a tenant and the landlord/city — Hey Neighbor seeks to bridge that gap.
The platform itself focus on serving data, which can be very dry. It was essential to portray warmness and humanity through the name, illustrations and branding.

Coney Island USA
Coney Island USA is an organization in Coney Island whose primary goal is to defend the honor of American popular culture. They do this by operating a museum and continuing the American tradition of sideshows. Since living in NYC, I've loved visiting Coney Island for its quirkiness, fantastic history, and beach. These things informed the design.

Art Direction
